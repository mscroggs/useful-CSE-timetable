This website is designed to help you navigate the SIAM CSE 2023 conference schedule. To get started, view the list of all speakers or the list of all talk titles. On both those pages, you can click a star next to any speaker/talk that you want to add to your favourites. Once you've done this, a list of all your favourited talks will appear on this page.

For example, if you click the star next to the name below, it will be added to your timetable. You can click the star again to remove the talk from your timetable.
